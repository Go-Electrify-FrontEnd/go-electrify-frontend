# Go-Electrify Wallet: How to Deposit Money

## Prerequisites

- You must be logged in to your Go-Electrify account.

## Step 1: Access Dashboard

Open the Go-Electrify dashboard by clicking the dashboard link in the main menu.

## Step 2: Navigate to Wallet

Click the **Wallet** option in the left sidebar of the dashboard.

## Step 3: Click Deposit Button

Click the **"Deposit"** button to open the deposit payment interface.

## Step 4: Enter Amount

Enter the amount of money you want to deposit into your wallet. The amount must be a valid number.

## Step 5: Initiate Payment

Click the **deposit button** to proceed. You will be redirected to the payment page.

## Step 6: Scan QR Code

Scan the QR code using your bank mobile app, Momo app, or other payment application to complete the payment.

## Step 7: Return to Wallet

After payment is complete, you will be automatically redirected to the Wallet page. Your balance should update immediately.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Money was deducted but not added to wallet

If your account was charged but the money did not appear in your wallet, contact support immediately:

- Email: **support@go-electrify.com**
- Alternatively, visit a charging station and speak with staff for immediate assistance.

### Problem: Payment failed or timed out

Try these steps:

1. Verify your internet connection is stable.
2. Check your bank account to confirm no duplicate charges.
3. Return to the wallet page and try again.
4. If the issue persists, contact support@go-electrify.com.

---

**Key Terms:**

- **Wallet:** Your Go-Electrify account balance for charging services.
- **Deposit:** Add money to your wallet for future charging sessions.
- **QR Code:** A visual code scanned by your phone to authorize payment.
