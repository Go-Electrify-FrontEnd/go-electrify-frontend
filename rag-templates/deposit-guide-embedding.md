# Deposit Money to Go-Electrify Wallet

**Quick Reference:** Add funds to your wallet for EV charging payments.

---

## Deposit Process

### Requirement

Must be logged in to your Go-Electrify account.

### Step 1: Open Dashboard

Access your Go-Electrify dashboard from the main menu.

### Step 2: Go to Wallet

Click **Wallet** in the left sidebar.

### Step 3: Start Deposit

Click the **Deposit** button.

### Step 4: Enter Amount

Type the amount you want to add to your wallet.

### Step 5: Confirm Payment

Click the deposit button to proceed to payment.

### Step 6: Authorize Payment

Scan the QR code with your bank app or Momo app to authorize the payment.

### Step 7: Wallet Updated

After payment succeeds, return to the Wallet page. Balance updates immediately.

---

## Issues & Solutions

### Balance Not Updated After Payment

**Symptom:** Money deducted but not added to wallet.
**Solution:** Contact support immediately at support@go-electrify.com or visit a charging station to speak with staff.

### Payment Failed or Timeout

**Symptom:** Payment did not go through.
**Steps:**

1. Check your internet connection.
2. Verify no duplicate charges on your bank account.
3. Try the deposit process again.
4. If still failing, contact support@go-electrify.com.

---

## FAQ

**Q: Is a login required to deposit?**
A: Yes, you must be logged in.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Bank transfer and Momo (via QR code scanning).

**Q: How long does a deposit take?**
A: Usually instant after authorization. Contact support if delayed beyond 5 minutes.

**Q: Can I deposit on behalf of another user?**
A: No, only the account holder can deposit to their wallet.

**Q: What is the minimum deposit amount?**
A: Refer to the payment system or contact support@go-electrify.com for limits.

---

**Tip:** Keep your wallet funded before heading to a charging station to ensure uninterrupted charging sessions.
