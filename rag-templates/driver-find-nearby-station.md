## Summary

This short guide explains how drivers locate nearby Go-Electrify charging stations using the mobile app and the public website. It shows searching, filtering by connector and availability, getting directions, and quick troubleshooting.

## Context

- **Topic:** Locating nearby charging stations
- **Goal:** Enable drivers to find a compatible and available charger quickly
- **Key Terms:**
  - **Availability** — Whether a charger is free or occupied

## Procedures

### Find nearby station (web)

- **When to Use:** Planning from a browser or laptop
- **Prerequisites:** Internet connection; optional sign-in for reservations and pricing
- **Steps:**
  1. Visit [Go-Electrify](https://www.go-electrify.com) and click "Find a Station".
  2. Allow location access or enter an address in the search field.
  3. Use filters to choose connector type and availability.
  4. Click a station marker and select "Get directions" to open your map application.

## Troubleshooting

### App doesn't show nearby stations

- **Symptoms:** Map empty or stations not visible
- **Resolution:**
  1. Verify location services are enabled and app permissions granted.
  2. Refresh the map or restart the app.
  3. Check network connectivity.
  4. Search an address manually if automatic detection fails.
  5. Report persistent issues via in-app support with screenshots.

### Station status incorrect

- **Symptoms:** Station marked occupied but appears free
- **Resolution:**
  1. Refresh station details and check last update timestamp.
  2. Wait 30–60 seconds and refresh again.
  3. If status is still wrong, contact [support@go-electrify.com](mailto:support@go-electrify.com) with station ID and photo.

## FAQ

- **Q:** Can I reserve a charger?
  - **A:** Yes, please navigate to the Reservation section on the left sidebar in the dashboard.

- **Q:** Why enable location?
  - **A:** Location access lets the app automatically center on your position and provide accurate navigation.
